4L Trophy - ECP Rapid'Occ

4L Trophy 2024 - ECP Rapid'Occ Crew 568

2023-2024

Partie 1 - Preparation

With my classmate Lino Lafore, we had decided to participate in the famous 4L Trophy, a unique humanitarian and sporting adventure 🌍.

The first step in our preparation had been the creation of the "ECP Rapid'Occ" association to structure our project and facilitate administrative procedures 📝.
After finding our car 🚗, we had to repair it to pass a rigorous technical inspection, ensuring it was in perfect condition to face the challenges of the Moroccan desert 🏜️.

Next, we undertook the search for sponsors to finance our adventure, making numerous contacts and presentations to convince companies to support us 💼.
In parallel, we created a LinkedIn page dedicated to our project (which can be found by clicking on the photo above), allowing us to share our progress, attract the attention of potential sponsors, and build a community around our adventure.

We were particularly proud to announce that we had succeeded in obtaining the support of Fabien Pelous, former player of the French rugby team and Stade Toulousain, as the sponsor of our project 🏉.

Partie 2 - Let's start !

Participating in the 4L Trophy was an unforgettable adventure 🌍.
Upon arriving in Morocco, I realized the magnitude of challenges facing local communities, we:

- Visited remote villages 🏘️,
- Distributed school supplies to children 📚,
- Met with dedicated local associations 🤝.

These experiences underscored the importance of mutual aid and solidarity.
Each encounter reminded me of the tangible impact we could make, fueling my commitment to further engage in humanitarian projects.

This journey also highlighted how even small actions can profoundly change lives 🌳.

Partie 3 - Debrief

After participating in the 4L Trophy, here are the key moments ✨ from this adventure:

- Mechanical preparation: We spent hours fixing our 4L to tackle the desert 🛠️. This taught me patience and the value of doing things right.
- Administrative challenges: Creating our association and securing sponsors was a real administrative challenge 💼. I learned the importance of organization and perseverance.
- Solidarity on the ground: Distributing school supplies to children and collaborating with local associations 📚 made me realize that every small action counts.
- Teamwork among participants: Helping and being helped by other teams during breakdowns created strong bonds 🚙.

In conclusion, this experience taught me the importance of solidarity, commitment, and teamwork.

These values will continue to inspire and guide me in all my future endeavors 🌍.

Federal tennis initiator

Federal tennis initiator - Toulouges Tennis Club (66)

2019

Partie 1 - What I learned

At 16, I completed a training program to become a "Federal Tennis Instructor," a certification for teaching tennis to children.
It was required to demonstrate a certain level of skill. 🏅
The one-week training taught me how to manage a group of kids and deliver effective lessons.
This endeavor was fully supported and encouraged by my tennis club. 🎾

Partie 2 - In practice !

As a tennis coach, I gained valuable experience and learned several key skills:

- Public speaking: Improved my ability to speak clearly and motivate others 🎤.
- Communication: Developed effective ways to interact with children and their parents 🤝.
- Importance of sports: Understood how sports contribute to education, teaching discipline and teamwork 🏅.
- Session planning: Learned to prepare engaging training sessions and select appropriate exercises 📝.
- Group management: Enhanced my skills in managing and organizing groups effectively 🎾.

These experiences have significantly boosted my coaching skills and overall confidence.
They've not only refined my technical abilities but also deepened my appreciation for the positive impact of sports on young lives.

This role has truly been a rewarding journey, blending personal growth with the joy of nurturing the next generation of athletes.🌍
